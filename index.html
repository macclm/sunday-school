MACCLM Quran and Arabic Classes Registration for summer Program 2022

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

MACCLM Sunday School is happy to announce running a “Summer Program” for our Muslim youngsters.

The program will start on Thursday, July 14 and end on August 12.The program will run on Thursdays and Fridays for 5 weeks .

We will offer three levels of Arabic classes and three levels of Quran classes.

There will be no homework assignments but enrichments of the understanding of the Quran and the Arabic language.

Every class will hold 10 sessions for a total fee of $70.00/class paid in advance.

Classes will run on Thursdays and Fridays for the first period from

6:30 -7:20 pm and second period from 7:30 - 8:20 pm followed by Magrib prayers.

Classes will start promptly at 6:30 so please drop off your children before that.,

Age Range and Levels:

Level 2 (7-8 years old),   Level 3 (9-12 years old) Teens (13-up)

Space is limited so if you are interested please stop by on Thursday at 6:00

To drop off your child, fill the registration form and pay the full fees.

Looking forward to see you.

  • I will send you soon the registration form to fill and bring with you.
